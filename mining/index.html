Positioning Solutions for Modern Mining Operations

Mining operations require reliable positioning in harsh conditions including dust, vibration, low visibility, remote sites and underground environments. Norvex combines GNSS, RTK, LPS, INS and PNT technologies to support safety, automation and operational efficiency.

RTK Vehicle Guidance & Safety

High-precision RTK GNSS positioning enables lane-level vehicle guidance for haul trucks and mining vehicles, especially in fog, dust, night operations and low-visibility conditions.

  • Route deviation alerts
  • Geofencing and hazard zones
  • Collision risk awareness
  • Centimeter-level vehicle positioning

GNSS Fleet Tracking & Logistics

Real-time GNSS tracking improves visibility across mining fleets, mobile assets and logistics workflows, helping operators monitor movement, utilization and productivity.

  • Fleet monitoring and dispatch
  • Asset and vehicle tracking
  • Material transport monitoring
  • Productivity and utilization analytics

Underground Positioning with LPS

In underground tunnels where GNSS signals are unavailable, local positioning systems based on UWB and LPS technologies can support tracking, safety alerts and situational awareness.

  • Personnel and vehicle tracking
  • Vehicle-to-person proximity warning
  • Emergency evacuation monitoring
  • GNSS-denied positioning

Machine Control & Excavation Assistance

RTK-based machine control supports excavation, grading, blade positioning and digital terrain workflows for more accurate and repeatable mining operations.

  • Blade and bucket positioning
  • Depth and slope monitoring
  • Operator guidance systems
  • Digital terrain model integration
